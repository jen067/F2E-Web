Fishing Net

Fishing nets are useful tools for fishermen to catch fish, allowing them to harvest seafood efficiently and sustainably. However, improper disposal of fishing nets can lead to ghost fishing, where discarded nets continue to trap marine life, harming ecosystems.

Plastic Bag

Plastic bags are common items in our daily lives, but they are a major source of pollution. When not disposed of properly, plastic bags can end up in waterways and oceans, harming marine life. Additionally, the production of plastic bags requires significant energy and resources, contributing to climate change.

Microplastic

Microplastics are plastic particles smaller than 5 millimeters. They are ubiquitous in the environment, from the deepest oceans to the highest mountains. Microplastics can be ingested by marine animals, leading to a variety of health problems, including impaired growth, reduced reproduction, and increased mortality.
